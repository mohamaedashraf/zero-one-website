Code Geist

CodeGeist is a hackathon organized by Sefr Wahed that will take place in August 2018. Focusing on undergrad students, teams of 5 will compete in making the most innovative, useful and robust product in 3 days of non-stop brainstorming and coding. Interact with other coders from around the country. Attend talks given by industry veterens. Get feedback from industry leaders and masterminds. Code. Create. Innovate.

Rules Sponsers

Registration Rules:

- The team must consist of undergrad students. Students that will graduated in 2018 may participate.
- Teams must consist of at least 3 members and no more than 5.
- Individuals looking to form teams and teams looking to recruit individuals will have the opportunity to do so through our Facebook community.

CodeGeist Rules:

- Sleeping over at the venue is permitted for male participants only. Transportation will be provided to transport participants back and forth.
- Harassment of participants, organizers, staff or any personell involved in the hackathon will not be tolerated in any way.
- Each team shall appoint one individual to represent and act on behalf of the team, including entering a Submission.
- All team members should be present at the event and all work on a project should be done at the hackathon.
- Teams can of course gain advice and support from organizers, volunteers, sponsors, and others.
- There are no limitations on the project ideas. You goal is to impress the judges.
- Teams can use libraries, frameworks, or open-source code in their projects. - Teams must stop hacking once the time is up.
